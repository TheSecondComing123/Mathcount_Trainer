# MathCounts Study Guide Website

A comprehensive study guide for MathCounts competitions, featuring in-depth topic explanations, contest format guides, strategies, and practice problems.

## Website Structure

### Main Pages
- **index.html** - Home page with overview of MathCounts and navigation
- **strategies/index.html** - Competition strategies and tips
- **practice/index.html** - Practice problems organized by topic and difficulty

### Topic Pages (topics/)
- **algebra.html** - Linear equations, quadratics, systems, polynomials, exponents
- **geometry.html** - 2D/3D shapes, triangles, circles, coordinate geometry
- **number-theory.html** - Primes, divisibility, GCD/LCM, modular arithmetic
- **counting-probability.html** - Permutations, combinations, probability
- **statistics.html** - Mean, median, mode, data analysis
- **arithmetic.html** - Fractions, decimals, percentages, ratios, mental math

### Contest Format Pages (contest-formats/)
- **sprint.html** - Sprint Round (30 problems, 40 minutes, no calculator)
- **target.html** - Target Round (8 problems in 4 pairs, calculator allowed)
- **countdown.html** - Countdown Round (head-to-head buzzer competition)
- **team.html** - Team Round (10 problems, 20 minutes, 4 team members)
