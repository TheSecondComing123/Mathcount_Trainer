Algebra Problems

Algebra #1: Easy
If 3x - 7 = 20, what is the value of x?
▶ Show Solution

Step 1: Add 7 to both sides

3x - 7 + 7 = 20 + 7

3x = 27

Step 2: Divide by 3

x = 27/3 = 9

Answer: x = 9

Algebra #2: Medium
Solve the system of equations: 2x + y = 13 and x - y = 5
▶ Show Solution

Using Addition Method:

Step 1: Add the equations to eliminate y

2x + y = 13

x - y = 5

──────────

3x = 18

Step 2: Solve for x

x = 6

Step 3: Substitute into either equation

6 - y = 5

y = 1

Answer: x = 6, y = 1

Algebra #3: Hard
If the roots of x² - kx + 12 = 0 are in the ratio 1:3, what is the value of k?
▶ Show Solution

Using Vieta's Formulas:

Step 1: Let roots be r and 3r

Step 2: Product of roots = 12

r × 3r = 12

3r² = 12

r² = 4

r = 2 (taking positive root)

Step 3: Sum of roots = k

r + 3r = k

2 + 6 = k

k = 8

Answer: k = 8

Geometry Problems

Geometry #1: Easy
A circle has a radius of 5 cm. What is its area in square centimeters? Express your answer in terms of π.
▶ Show Solution

Apply the area formula:

Formula: A = πr²

Step 1: Substitute r = 5

A = π(5)²

A = 25π

Answer: 25π cm²

Geometry #2: Medium
In a right triangle, one leg is 8 cm and the hypotenuse is 17 cm. What is the length of the other leg in cm?
▶ Show Solution

Use Pythagorean Theorem:

Formula: a² + b² = c²

Step 1: Substitute known values

8² + b² = 17²

64 + b² = 289

Step 2: Solve for b

b² = 289 - 64 = 225

b = 15

Note: This is the 8-15-17 Pythagorean triple!

Answer: 15 cm

Geometry #3: Hard
Two similar triangles have areas of 25 and 100 square units. If the smaller triangle has a perimeter of 20 units, what is the perimeter of the larger triangle?
▶ Show Solution

Use similar triangle properties:

Step 1: Find ratio of areas

Area ratio = 25:100 = 1:4

Step 2: Find ratio of corresponding sides

If area ratio is a²:b², then side ratio is a:b

Side ratio = √1:√4 = 1:2

Step 3: Perimeter has same ratio as sides

Perimeter ratio = 1:2

20:x = 1:2

x = 40

Answer: 40 units

Number Theory Problems

Number Theory #1: Easy
What is the greatest common divisor (GCD) of 24 and 36?
▶ Show Solution

Method 1: Prime Factorization

Step 1: Factor both numbers

24 = 2³ × 3

36 = 2² × 3²

Step 2: Take lowest powers of common primes

GCD = 2² × 3 = 4 × 3 = 12

Answer: 12

Number Theory #2: Medium
What is the units digit of 7^2024?
▶ Show Solution

Find the pattern in units digits:

Step 1: Calculate powers of 7

7¹ ends in 7

7² ends in 9

7³ ends in 3

7⁴ ends in 1

7⁵ ends in 7 (pattern repeats)

Step 2: Pattern has cycle of 4

2024 ÷ 4 = 506 remainder 0

Step 3: Remainder 0 means units digit is same as 7⁴

Units digit = 1

Answer: 1

Number Theory #3: Hard
How many positive integers less than 100 are relatively prime to 30? (Two numbers are relatively prime if their GCD is 1.)
▶ Show Solution

Use Euler's Totient Function:

Step 1: Factor 30

30 = 2 × 3 × 5

Step 2: Apply totient formula

φ(30) = 30 × (1 - 1/2) × (1 - 1/3) × (1 - 1/5)

= 30 × 1/2 × 2/3 × 4/5

= 30 × 8/30 = 8

Step 3: Count multiples of 8 less than 100

In each group of 30: 8 numbers are relatively prime

Groups: 1-30, 31-60, 61-90

Total = 3 × 8 = 24

Plus 91-99: check 91, 97 (both relatively prime)

Total = 24 + 2 = 26

Answer: 26

Counting & Probability Problems

Counting #1: Easy
How many ways can 3 books be arranged on a shelf?
▶ Show Solution

This is a permutation (order matters):

Method 1: Factorial

3! = 3 × 2 × 1 = 6

Method 2: Counting Principle

First position: 3 choices

Second position: 2 choices

Third position: 1 choice

Total = 3 × 2 × 1 = 6

Answer: 6 ways

Counting #2: Medium
A committee of 3 people is to be chosen from a group of 7 people. How many different committees can be formed?
▶ Show Solution

This is a combination (order doesn't matter):

Formula: C(n,r) = n!/(r!(n-r)!)

Step 1: Apply formula

C(7,3) = 7!/(3!4!)

Step 2: Simplify

= (7 × 6 × 5)/(3 × 2 × 1)

= 210/6 = 35

Answer: 35 committees

Probability #1: Medium
A fair coin is flipped three times. What is the probability of getting exactly 2 heads?
▶ Show Solution

Step 1: Find total outcomes

2³ = 8 possible outcomes

Step 2: Count favorable outcomes

Exactly 2 heads: HHT, HTH, THH

3 favorable outcomes

Step 3: Calculate probability

P = 3/8

Alternative: Using combinations

C(3,2) × (1/2)² × (1/2)¹ = 3 × 1/8 = 3/8

Answer: 3/8

Arithmetic & Statistics Problems

Arithmetic #1: Easy
What is 35% of 60?
▶ Show Solution

Method 1: Convert to decimal

35% = 0.35

0.35 × 60 = 21

Method 2: Mental math

10% of 60 = 6

30% of 60 = 18

5% of 60 = 3

35% = 30% + 5% = 18 + 3 = 21

Answer: 21

Statistics #1: Medium
The mean of five numbers is 18. If one of the numbers is 22, what is the mean of the other four numbers?
▶ Show Solution

Step 1: Find sum of all five numbers

Mean = Sum/Count

18 = Sum/5

Sum = 90

Step 2: Subtract the known number

Sum of other 4 = 90 - 22 = 68

Step 3: Find new mean

Mean of 4 numbers = 68/4 = 17

Answer: 17

Mixed Practice - Sprint Round Simulation

Sprint #1: Easy
Simplify: 2/3 + 1/6
▶ Show Solution

2/3 = 4/6

4/6 + 1/6 = 5/6

Answer: 5/6

Sprint #2: Medium
The perimeter of a square is 36 cm. What is its area in square centimeters?
▶ Show Solution

Perimeter = 4s = 36

s = 9

Area = s² = 81

Answer: 81 cm²
