About MathCounts

MathCounts is a national middle school mathematics competition designed for students in grades 6-8. The competition emphasizes problem-solving skills, mathematical reasoning, and computational speed across various mathematical topics.

Competition Levels

MathCounts competitions are held at four levels:

  • School Competition - First level, held at individual schools
  • Chapter Competition - Regional level for top performers
  • State Competition - State-wide competition for chapter winners
  • National Competition - Top students from each state compete for the national championship

Difficulty Progression

Problems generally increase in difficulty: School < Chapter < State < National

Prepare for higher levels by practicing problems from the next competition tier.

Contest Formats

Each MathCounts competition consists of four distinct rounds, each testing different skills and abilities.

Sprint Round

30 Problems 40 Minutes No Calculator

Focus: Speed and accuracy

Test your computational skills and quick problem-solving abilities. Each problem is worth 1 point.

Key Strategy: The first 20 problems are typically easier. Scan through all problems and tackle your strengths first.

Learn More

Target Round

8 Problems (4 Pairs) 6 Min per Pair Calculator Allowed

Focus: Problem-solving and mathematical reasoning

More challenging problems requiring deeper analysis. Each problem is worth 2 points.

Key Strategy: Use the full 6 minutes for each pair. Always check your work before submitting.

Learn More

Team Round

10 Problems 20 Minutes Calculator Allowed

Focus: Collaboration and problem-solving

Work with your team of 4 students to solve challenging problems. Each problem is worth 2 points.

Key Strategy: Divide problems based on team strengths. Communicate effectively and double-check answers.

Learn More

Countdown Round

Head-to-Head 45 Sec per Problem No Calculator

Focus: Speed under pressure

Face off against another competitor with buzzer-based competition. The ultimate test of speed and accuracy.

Note: This round determines the National Champion at MathCounts Nationals. It's optional at lower levels.

Learn More

Scoring System

Individual Score = Sprint Round Score + Target Round Score

  • Sprint: 30 problems × 1 point = 30 points maximum
  • Target: 8 problems × 2 points = 16 points maximum
  • Total Individual Score: 46 points maximum

Team Score = Sum of top 4 individual scores + Team Round score
